About this channel

The Kitchen Edit is a Pinterest Business account publishing daily kitchen and cooking content to a UK audience. Pinterest is a major driver of kitchen appliance discovery — people planning kitchen upgrades, searching for birthday gift ideas, and researching their next coffee machine all use it as a starting point.

The channel focuses on premium and aspirational products where visual appeal is strong and purchase intent is high. Air fryers, stand mixers and coffee machines are among the most-searched kitchen categories on Pinterest. We publish content targeting those searches daily.
